Practical Task

Perform keyword research for 10 blog topics

Find 10 low-competition keywords your new blog can realistically rank for.
Step-by-Step Method
  1. Open Ubersuggest or Google Keyword Planner and enter your niche topic.
  2. Search for keywords with monthly volume between 500 and 5,000.
  3. Filter for keywords with low SEO difficulty (under 30 on most tools).
  4. Check the top 5 ranking pages for each keyword — can you write better content?
  5. Create a spreadsheet with keyword, monthly volume, difficulty and topic title.
DeliverableA spreadsheet of 10 research-backed blog topics ready to write.
Pro TipTarget keywords where the top-ranking articles are weak, short or outdated.
Practical Task

Write and SEO-optimize a full blog article

Practice writing an article with proper on-page SEO structure.
Step-by-Step Method
  1. Choose one keyword from your research and write a 1,000-word article.
  2. Include the keyword in the title, first paragraph, one H2 heading and conclusion.
  3. Add 3 to 5 internal links to other pages on your blog.
  4. Write a meta description of 150 to 160 characters with the keyword.
  5. Add at least one image with a descriptive alt text containing the keyword.
Deliverable1 complete SEO-optimized article published or ready to publish.
Pro TipMatch search intent — if people want a list, write a list. If they want a how-to, write a guide.
Practical Task

Set up Google Search Console for a blog

Learn how to submit your site and track SEO performance.
Step-by-Step Method
  1. Create a Google Search Console account and add your domain.
  2. Verify ownership using the HTML tag method in your WordPress site.
  3. Submit your sitemap URL (usually yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml).
  4. Check the Coverage report to confirm pages are being indexed.
  5. Review the Performance tab to see any early keyword impressions.
DeliverableA verified Search Console setup with sitemap submitted and index confirmed.
Pro TipSearch Console is free — it is the most important SEO tool for bloggers and completely free.
