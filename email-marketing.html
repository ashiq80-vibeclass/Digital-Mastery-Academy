📧 Email Marketing

Build email lists, clean lead data, write high-converting campaigns, and create automated email systems using Brevo. Turn email into a consistent source of traffic and sales.

Course Overview

What You Will Learn

  • Build and manage permission-based email lists for businesses and brands.
  • Clean email data in Google Sheets to improve deliverability and reduce bounce rates.
  • Write effective email campaigns with strong subject lines and clear calls-to-action.
  • Set up automated email sequences using Brevo for lead nurturing and sales.

Structured Path

Beginner to Professional — 3 Phases

Follow this structured path from complete beginner to professional-level skill. Each phase builds on the previous one.

Phase 1 — Beginner

  • What email marketing is and how it fits into digital business
  • Types of emails — newsletters, promotions, automations
  • Setting up a Brevo account and verified sending domain

Phase 2 — Intermediate

  • Cleaning email data in Google Sheets — removing duplicates, invalid formats
  • Writing subject lines that improve open rates

Phase 3 — Professional

  • Setting up automated welcome and nurture sequences
  • A/B testing subject lines and email content
  • Analyzing campaign reports — open rate, CTR and unsubscribes
  • Building a complete email system for a client or business

Practical Task

Clean a list of 100 email addresses in Google Sheets

Practice list hygiene to improve deliverability before any campaign.
Step-by-Step Method
  1. Open a sample spreadsheet with 100 email addresses.
  2. Remove all obvious duplicates using the Remove Duplicates feature.
  3. Use a formula or filter to find emails missing the @ symbol or domain.
  4. Mark all addresses with obvious spam patterns such as test@test.com.
  5. Export a clean final list and count how many valid emails remain.
DeliverableA cleaned email spreadsheet with a final count of valid versus removed entries.
Pro TipA clean list always outperforms a large dirty list. Deliverability is more important than list size.
Practical Task

Write a 3-email welcome sequence

Learn to structure an automated onboarding sequence for new subscribers.
Step-by-Step Method
  1. Write Email 1 — a warm welcome that delivers on whatever the subscriber signed up for.
  2. Write Email 2 — sent 2 days later — share one valuable tip or insight.
  3. Write Email 3 — sent on day 5 — introduce your offer or ask for a reply.
  4. Write a subject line for each email under 50 characters.
  5. Set up the 3 emails in Brevo as an automation triggered by a new subscriber.
DeliverableA 3-email automation sequence live in Brevo with subject lines and scheduled delays.
Pro TipPeople decide whether to stay subscribed within the first 3 emails. Make each one worth opening.
Practical Task

Design and send a promotional email campaign

Practice the full workflow from design to send.
Step-by-Step Method
  1. Create a new campaign in Brevo and choose a simple single-column template.
  2. Write a subject line, headline, 2 short body paragraphs and one CTA button.
  3. Preview the email on desktop and mobile view before sending.
  4. Send the campaign to a test list of 5 addresses and check rendering.
  5. After 24 hours, check the campaign report for open rate and click rate.
DeliverableA sent campaign with a screenshot of the report showing open and click data.
Pro TipTest everything before sending to a real list. One broken link can ruin an entire campaign.
