Phase 3 — Professional

Practical Task

Edit a 30-second motivational reel

Understand timeline, cuts, text placement and music sync.
Step-by-Step Method
  1. Open your software and create a 1080×1920 vertical project.
  2. Import 3 to 5 short clips and arrange them on the timeline.
  3. Cut slow or weak moments and keep only the strongest parts.
  4. Add one motivational text hook in the first 2 seconds.
  5. Add background music and reduce its volume under any voice track.
  6. Export in MP4 and review pacing before saving the final version.
Deliverable1 completed vertical reel with text and music.
Pro TipKeep the first 3 seconds strong. That is where attention is won or lost.
Practical Task

Remove silence from a talking-head clip

Learn audio cleanup and pacing improvement techniques.
Step-by-Step Method
  1. Import one talking-head video clip into the timeline.
  2. Listen carefully and identify all silent gaps and filler pauses.
  3. Cut dead space between sentences and join clips cleanly.
  4. Add zoom cuts or B-roll inserts if jump cuts feel too abrupt.
  5. Check audio consistency across the full clip and export a clean version.
DeliverableA before-and-after comparison showing the cleaned edit.
Pro TipDo not over-cut. The speaker must still feel natural and conversational.
Practical Task

Create a 5-minute YouTube edit

Combine intro, main content, captions and outro in one complete workflow.
Step-by-Step Method
  1. Prepare an intro card, main footage clips and an outro screen.
  2. Trim all visible mistakes and arrange sections in logical order.
  3. Add subtitles to important lines and visually emphasize key moments.
  4. Use simple transitions only where content changes topic or location.
  5. Balance audio levels throughout, export in 1080p and save source project.
Deliverable1 YouTube-ready video with intro, captions and outro.
Pro TipProfessional editing feels clean and purposeful — not over-decorated.
